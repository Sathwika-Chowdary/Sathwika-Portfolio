# Reporting Notes

## Recommended KPIs
- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate %
- Average Monthly Charges
- Average Tenure

## Recommended Segment Breakdowns
- Contract type
- Payment method
- Internet service
- Senior citizen status
- Tenure bands

## Dashboard / Reporting Views
1. **Executive Overview**
   - Churn Rate %
   - Total Customers
   - Churned Customers

2. **Segment Analysis**
   - Churn by Contract
   - Churn by Payment Method
   - Churn by Internet Service

3. **Customer Value / Risk**
   - Monthly Charges by Churn
   - Tenure by Churn

## Business Interpretation
This reporting layer should highlight which customer groups are more likely to churn and where retention efforts should be targeted.
